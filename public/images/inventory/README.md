# Inventory Images

1. `flatbed-kubota-loader.jpg` - White flatbed tow truck with orange Kubota SVL 75 compact track loader
2. `tow-truck-bus.jpg` - White flatbed tow truck transporting white bus
3. `flatbed-trailers.jpg` - White flatbed tow truck with two white enclosed trailers
4. `tow-truck-minibus.jpg` - White tow truck transporting white mini-bus
5. `kubota-side-view.jpg` - Side view of white flatbed tow truck with orange Kubota compact track loader

**Note:** If images are not found, the component will automatically show placeholder images.
